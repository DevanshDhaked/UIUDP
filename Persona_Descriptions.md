# User Personas – Pregnancy Tracking Application

This document contains detailed descriptions of the 16 user personas for the pregnancy tracking application, spanning diverse geographic, economic, and cultural contexts across India, the United States, the United Kingdom, Mexico, Ghana, Japan, Germany, and Brazil.

---

## Persona 1: The Analytical Metro Professional

**Demographics**
- Name: Priya Sharma
- Age: 31
- Location: Mumbai, India (Tier 1) – Prefers English
- Income: ₹18,000,000/year
- Education: Master's in Business Administration (MBA)

**Psychographics**
- Personality traits: Highly organized, data-driven, slightly anxious
- Motivations: Wants precise, day-by-day fetal development updates and actionable dietary guidelines tailored to an Indian vegetarian diet.
- Fears: Missing a crucial milestone or symptom because of conflicting advice online; "toxic positivity" in mom groups.
- Attitudes: Skeptical of AI chatbots for serious symptoms; strictly prefers doctor-validated content but loves AI for meal planning and organizing checklists.

**Behavior**
- Daily routine: Busy corporate job, commutes via cab, squeezes in reading during transit.
- Tech habits: Extremely tech-savvy. Uses premium, paid apps to avoid ads.
- Media consumption: Listens to pregnancy podcasts, reads medical blogs (e.g., Mayo Clinic, WebMD).

**Decision Model**
- Triggers: Experiencing a new physical symptom during the workday prompts immediate searches.
- Barriers: Cluttered UI, unverified medical claims, and apps that don't allow data export for her obstetrician.
- Priorities: Doctor-backed information, clean visuals, and strict privacy policies.
