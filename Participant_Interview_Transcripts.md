**Interviewer:** What do you do when you see medical words you do not know?

**Priya:** I search for the term on the WebMD website. I look for a definition and a list of related symptoms. I also check if my pregnancy app has a glossary. I write the word in my digital notes for my next doctor visit. I do not trust information from forums.

**Interviewer:** Show me how you track your pregnancy progress.

**Priya:** I open my premium pregnancy tracker on my phone. I log my weight and any symptoms into the app daily. I also use a private spreadsheet on my laptop. **Interviewer:** Describe your experience using AI tools like ChatGPT or Gemini for pregnancy questions.
